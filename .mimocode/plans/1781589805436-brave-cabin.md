## Issue 2: Accessibility Contrast Failures (Issue #579)

**Root Cause**: Semantic design tokens (`text-foreground`, `text-gray-500`, `bg-background`) resolve to very light colors in light mode that don't meet WCAG 2 AA 4.5:1 minimum contrast ratio.

**Affected Pages**:
- Admin grants page: 8 elements with contrast ratios as low as 1.37
- Reader settings panel: 10+ elements with contrast ratios as low as 1.07

**Failing Elements**:
- `<h1>` heading ("Manage Book Access")
- `<p>` description text (`text-gray-500`)
- `<button>` ("Back to Books" with `text-primary-600`)
- `<select>` (language selector with `bg-background`)
- `<label>` ("Book" label with `text-gray-700`)
- Reader settings: theme buttons, font size buttons, labels

**Fix Required**:
1. Audit `globals.css` for light-mode token definitions
2. Update light-mode variants of `text-foreground`, `text-gray-500`, `text-primary-600`, `bg-background` to meet 4.5:1 contrast
3. Verify changes with axe-core or manual contrast checker
