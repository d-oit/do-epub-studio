# 000: Product Overview

## Purpose
`do EPUB Studio` is a web-based EPUB reader and editorial workspace for self-publishing, controlled distribution, and annotated review.

## Core Features
- EPUB reading and offline PWA
- Gated access by email (optional password)
- Public or private distribution
- Bookmarks, highlights, and threaded comments
- Audit logging and permission management

## Target Users
- Authors sharing manuscript EPUBs with selected readers
- Editors reviewing EPUBs with comments and discussion
- Proofreaders accessing protected drafts
- Readers with read-only access

## Success Criteria
- One private EPUB, one approved reader grant, one successful authenticated reading session, and one offline-capable resume flow.
